1
Go to
https://web-get-system-info.onrender.com/download
2
Click on the download button (latest version)
3
After clicking on download, you will be redirected to a drive security page
Click on "Download anyway" - it is safe to use and does not contain any virus
4
After clicking on "download anyway", a zip file will start to download named "dist.rar"
5
After the file is downloaded, click on "show in folder" Once you click on "show in folder", the location of the zip file will open up. Now right click on the file and click on "extract file" like this: After clicking "extract file", you will see a popup to choose the location. 6
Now the file is extracted and you will see a folder named "dist". Click on that folder. After opening the folder you will see:
